McLean Hall

❮ ❯

McLean Hall houses most of the engineering and science classes on campus. It also has many active labs including a few for biomedical engineering and chemistry.

Navigation:

McLean Hall is located directly across from the River Street side of the Edwin A. Stevens (EAS) building. It is one of the farthest buildings on campus, like EAS. The fastest way to get to this building from off campus is to walk up 4th Street all the way to River Street. If you are on campus, either walking the back athletic parking lot pathway down River Street or walking towards Babbio then turning right to River Street then turning left until you see the McLean building sign.
